About Naruto

A young orphan boy born to the shinobi wolrd in the village known as konohagakure (The village hidden in the leafs), his parents died during his child birth so he could live, before his parent died to save the village and their son they sealed a nine tailed demon fox within him with a powerful seal called the eight trigrams seal, in better hopes for the future. He was kept in the care of the third hokage called Hiruzen Sarutobi, his shinobi journey started without parental love and care, but with his strong will to fulfill his goal of being the strongest shinobi/hokage of the hidden leaf village he survived and endured life circumstances under the instructions of those who cared about him, Jiraiya Sensei, Iruka Sensei, Kakashi Hatake Sensei and he got the love and respect he deserved from his lover, friends and everyone in the village.

At the end he was able to save his childhood friend, saved the village from the attack of a strong villian, ended the fourth ninja war, become the hokage and get married to his lover from childhood and bore two kids, Himawari and Boruto. He brought peace to the entire ninja world and the nations became allied and lived together in harmony
